**Do’s**

* Do adjust your privacy settings to ensure only friends and family see your personal posts and photos
* Do think twice before posting. If you wouldn't say it to a professor's face, don't post it online.
* Do fact-check news or viral "warnings" before sharing them to avoid spreading misinformation.
* Do use social media to network professionally on platforms like LinkedIn to build your future career.
* Do take regular "digital detox" breaks to protect your mental health and focus on your studies.





**Don'ts**

* Don’t post your location, home address, or phone number in public captions or stories.
* Don’t engage with "trolls" or participate in cyberbullying, even if you feel you are in the right.
* Don’t post photos of others without their explicit permission, especially in private settings.
* Don’t complain about your college, internships, or future employers on public forums.
* Don’t click on suspicious links or "free giveaway" ads that pop up in your feed or DMs.
